What did the news said?

Wordcloud of news dataset(titles), could represent each term's importance and frequency, the bigger words means greater weight.

The following will explain several essential wordings in the dataset.

  • Covid: Main theme of the dataset.
  • Lockdown: Frequent ambushed lockdowns in residential areas for compulsory virus test adopted in Hong Kong; Lockdowns in different countries or cities (e.g. sealing boarder, suspend classes and works) to prevent virus spread.
  • China: Covid-19 cases in China; WHO invetigates virus origin in China; How China helps other countries to control virus spread.
  • Vaccine: Invention and testing of covid-19 vaccines; Vaccination situation in different countries or cities.
  • Govt: Short term of Government; How different country's government handle the pandemic.
  • Test: Situation of compulsory virus test in Hong Kong for specific industries or lockdown areas.
